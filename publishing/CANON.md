## Axioms

### 1. Dissemination Stack

Every paper **MUST** disseminate through the complete stack:

| Layer | Platform | Purpose | Evidence |
|-------|----------|---------|----------|
| **Preprint** | arXiv / medRxiv | Timestamp + Open Access | DOI |
| **Professional** | LinkedIn | Network + Credibility | Post URL |
| **Social** | Twitter/X | Reach + Virality | Tweet URL |
| **Company** | Company Page | Official Record | Blog URL |

Incomplete dissemination = incomplete publication.

---

### 2. Platform Routing

Papers route to domain-appropriate preprint servers:

| Sublanguage | Preprint Server | Domain |
|-------------|-----------------|--------|
| CANONIC (root) | arXiv | Computer Science / AI |
| CANONIC.MEDICINE | medRxiv | Healthcare / Clinical |
| CANONIC.LAW | SSRN | Legal / Policy |
| CANONIC.FINANCE | SSRN | Economics / Finance |

Wrong platform = invalid publication.

---

### 3. Cross-Registration

Publications **MUST** cross-register across platforms:

- **arXiv ↔ medRxiv** — Cross-list for interdisciplinary work
- **LinkedIn → Twitter** — Synchronized announcement
- **Company → All** — Official record links to all platforms

Cross-registration creates evidence mesh.

---

### 4. Publication Sequence

Publications **MUST** follow temporal sequence:

```
1. Preprint (timestamp authority)
2. LinkedIn (professional network)
3. Twitter/X (viral reach)
4. Company (official record)
```

Sequence violations break evidence chain.
