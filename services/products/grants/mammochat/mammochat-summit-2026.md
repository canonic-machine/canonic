# MammoChat Summit 2026

**First CANONIC Breast Cancer Meeting for Patients**

**Date:** October 2026
**Location:** Orlando, FL (Lake Nona)
**Host:** MammoChat™ Foundation (Not-for-profit)

---

## Vision

Community-focused breast cancer support summit. Patients first. No university politics.

---

## Format

Based on UCF AI in Medicine Summit layout:

| Session | Focus | Audience |
|---------|-------|----------|
| Keynotes | AI in breast cancer care | All |
| Breakouts | Survivorship, treatment, support | Patients & families |
| Demos | MammoChat platform launch | All |
| Poster Session | Patient stories & research | Community |
| Networking | Peer connections | Patients & advocates |

---

## Differences from UCF AIMS

| UCF AIMS | MammoChat Summit |
|----------|------------------|
| Academic focus | Patient focus |
| UCF-hosted | Foundation-hosted |
| Physician audience | Patient & family audience |
| Research presentations | Lived experience sharing |
| University politics | Community-driven |

## Alignment with Grant Requirements

Per FDOH FCIF Grant 354:

> "it is expected that any results or outcomes are shared publicly to further Florida's cancer research innovation agenda"

MammoChat Summit 2026 fulfills this requirement through:
- Public platform launch
- Community engagement
- Patient education
- Open source demonstration
