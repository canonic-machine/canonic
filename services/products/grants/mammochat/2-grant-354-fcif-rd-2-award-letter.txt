Dear Dr. Dexter Hadley,

I am pleased to inform you that grant application 354 for the project titled MammoChat: An AI-Driven Platform for Personalized Breast Cancer Patient Support and submitted to the Casey DeSantis Florida Cancer Innovation Fund has been approved for the amount requested, $1,998,935. On behalf of the Florida Department of Health (Department), congratulations on this well-deserved achievement.

Before the Department will issue the grant for execution, the grantee must submit regulatory approvals including certification of Institutional Review Board approval and Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee approval to the Department to ensure expeditious innovation in cancer research.

The Department will be preparing a contract that outlines the terms and conditions of the grant, which will be sent to you and your listed business official. Please review the contract thoroughly. Please note as stated in the terms and conditions, that to accept these funds and by signing the contract, all research funded under this opportunity must be conducted in Florida. Additionally, in accepting grant funds, the awardee and awardee's institution agree that any work, invention, discovery, or development resulting from the grant-funded project shall be subject to public domain, and not be subject to patents, trademarks, and/or copyrights that could result in financial gain to the awardee or awardee's institution without prior approval from the Department. Further, it is expected that any results or outcomes are shared publicly to further Florida's cancer research innovation agenda.

If you have any questions or require clarification, do not hesitate to reach out to the Department's grant administration team via email at FloridaCancerInnovationFund@flhealth.gov. Competitive grant awards are subject to the availability of funds.

Your commitment to advancing cancer research and innovation is commendable. The Department is pleased to support this important work and confident that your project will have a meaningful impact in the fight against cancer.

Sincerely,

Lauren Cassedy
