The artifact being validated in this example. Contains exactly "Hello, world." to demonstrate the simplest possible governed artifact.

### exact match validation
Syntactic validation where the artifact content must match the constraint specification character-for-character. Demonstrates binary pass/fail validation with zero ambiguity.
